ABOUT WANDERLO

Your one-step solution for travel planning. Expert guidance for your journey to the world's most amazing destinations.

Our Mission

Wanderlo is an AI-powered budget trip planner designed to revolutionize how students and young travelers explore the world. We combine cutting-edge technology with local expertise to create personalized, authentic, and affordable travel experiences.

Our platform connects travelers with local guides who offer unique insights and experiences beyond typical tourist attractions. We believe that travel should be accessible, enriching, and transformative.

Why Choose Wanderlo

Budget-Friendly

We specialize in creating amazing travel experiences that won't break the bank, perfect for students and young travelers.

Local Experts

Our guides are locals who know their destinations intimately and can show you hidden gems you won't find in guidebooks.

Personalized Plans

AI-powered planning creates customized itineraries based on your preferences, budget, and travel style.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of travel enthusiasts and tech experts, Wanderlo was born from a shared frustration with the one-size-fits-all approach of traditional travel agencies and the impersonal nature of online booking platforms.

We set out to create a service that combines the best of technology and human expertise to deliver truly personalized travel experiences. Today, we're proud to help thousands of travelers discover the world in a more authentic, affordable, and meaningful way.
