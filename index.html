ABOUT WANDERLO

Your one-step solution for travel planning. Expert guidance for your journey to the world's most amazing destinations.

Our Mission

Wanderlo is an AI-powered budget trip planner designed to revolutionize how students and young travelers explore the world. We combine cutting-edge technology with local expertise to create personalized, authentic, and affordable travel experiences.

Our platform connects travelers with verified local guides, helps discover hidden gems, and creates meaningful travel connections through our innovative social features.

Budget-Friendly

Custom travel plans based on your budget, time, and preferences with dynamic cost optimization using local data.

Verified Guides

All our local guides and vendors are thoroughly vetted and rated to ensure authentic and safe experiences.

How It Works

Wanderlo uses advanced AI algorithms to create personalized travel itineraries that match your preferences and budget. Our platform integrates with multiple APIs to provide real-time information on accommodations, transportation, and local experiences.

AI Planning

Our AI analyzes your preferences to create optimized travel plans with the best routes, stays, and experiences.

Local Discovery

Discover hidden gems and local markets that typical tourists miss, with geo-tagged recommendations.

Social Integration

Share your travel stories, connect with other travelers, and build a community of travel enthusiasts.

Partnerships

We collaborate with local vendors, hotels, and experience providers to bring you the best travel options at affordable prices. Our partners are carefully selected to ensure quality and authenticity.

Accommodation Partners

From budget hostels to boutique hotels, we partner with accommodations that offer the best value.

Local Experiences

Authentic culinary experiences, workshops, and cultural activities with local experts.

Our Help Center provides comprehensive assistance for all aspects of using Wanderlo. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact our support team.

How do I book a trip?

To book a trip, simply search for your destination, select your preferred dates, and choose from available hotels and experiences. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Some regions may have additional payment options available.
